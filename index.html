About Us

Welcome to TAMPEPE & PASAJE KAINAN, where we bring the best of Filipino cuisine to your table. Our cozy ambiance and warm hospitality create the perfect place to enjoy traditional dishes made with the finest ingredients.

Our Menu

  • Adobo - Tender pork or chicken in a savory soy-vinegar sauce.
  • Sinigang na Baboy - Pork in a tangy tamarind-based soup.
  • Pancit Canton - Stir-fried noodles with vegetables and shrimp.
  • Lechon Kawali - Crispy fried pork belly served with dipping sauce.
  • Halo-Halo - A sweet, colorful dessert with shaved ice, leche flan, and fruits.
